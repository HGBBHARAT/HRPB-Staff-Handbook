THINGS TO CONSIDER

✔ Rule 1: Staff are required to utilize spelling, punctuation and grammar at all times. (This includes capitalizing the start of sentences and putting periods at the ends of sentences).

✔ Rule 2: Staff must always maintain professionalism and respect for others.

✗ Rule 3: Staff cannot abuse the :h and :m commands. Only use these commands for announcements (e.g. `:h Blocking border lanes or honking and ramming at the border will result in moderation` ✅ `:h Mod online`❌).

✔ Rule 4: Staff must always use four or more letter commands. (e.g. `:view Jydx` ✅ `:kick jX` is not permitted).

✔ Rule 5: Staff must respond to mod calls. Failure to do so is considered a neglect of duty. You signed up for this job and you have to fulfill every responsibility.

✗ Rule 6: Staff may NOT use kill-logs as proof in an RDM situation. We only take clips, screenshots, and recordings as proof.

✔ Rule 7: Staff must always use correct uniforms and liveries.

✔ Rule 8: All staff should know the rules and review all staff documents (Punishment Section).

✔ Rule 9: Staff must have the correct callsign including their rank, and random numbers. (e.g. Junior Mod = JM-120 or Senior Admin = SA-01).

✗ Rule 10: Staff may not go AFK on duty. If you are on duty, you should be moderating—not standing and doing nothing.

✔ Rule 11: Staff must take proper measures to maintain a safe environment for all members.

✗ Rule 12: Staff cannot be disrespectful to anyone—including members. SHR and HR disrespect will lead to logged warnings.

✔ Rule 13: Staff must use common sense. Just because a rule isn't on here, doesn't mean you can do it. Think before you do something. (e.g. Trolling).

✗ Rule 14: Staff cannot abuse their commands and use it in an unfair way that ruins the roleplay experience.

✗ Rule 15: Staff cannot use commands off duty unless you have the off-duty commands role or are ownership+.

Any staff member who violates any of these rules will be immediately TERMINATED. IT IS THE RESPONSIBILITY OF EACH STAFF MEMBER TO READ THIS DOCUMENT.

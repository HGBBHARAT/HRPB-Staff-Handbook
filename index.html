ERLC Private Server Rules

Rule 1 | FRP: All roleplays must withhold a realistic standard and you must not do anything that could be deemed as unrealistic.
Rule 2 | VDM: Vehicle deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot other vehicles without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 3 | RDM: Random Deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot or kill anyone without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 4 | Gun Motion: You must use Gun Motion such as "grabs gun" or "slings" for every weapon apart from the pistol or taser. Law enforcement do not need to use gun motion at all.
Rule 5 | Realistic Avatars: Any morphs of your heads, bodies etc are not allowed.
Rule 6 | New Life Rule: Once your character is arrested or dies then you have no previous member as that character.
Rule 7 | Team Creation: Smaller teams like CIA, FBI etc. isn't allowed. Apply in discord.
Rule 8 | Realistic Driving: Drive realistically and roleplay all of your crashes no matter the speed.
Rule 9 | Fear RP: If someone is pointing a weapon at you, you must comply with their demands.
Rule 10 | Detain Requests: You must always accept detain requests. If unjustified, call staff afterwards.
Rule 11 | Pointless Open Carrying: Carrying a gun around for attention isn't allowed.
Rule 12 | Cop Baiting: Strictly not allowed. Do not start pointless pursuits.
Rule 13 | Robberies: 5 Minute Wait Time. Bank robberies require staff permission.
Rule 14 | Interrupting Roleplays: Do not interfere with on-going role plays.
Rule 15 | Priorities: No criminal activity that makes you wanted during priorities.
Rule 16 | Initiating Pursuits: Must always have a valid reason to initiate a pursuit.
Rule 17 | Group Limits: Max 4 for crimes, max 3 for Jewelry store.
Rule 18 | SWAT Gear: Not allowed if you aren't trained in SWAT.
Rule 19 | VC: If VC startup is selected, you must be in Discord VC.
Rule 20 | Safe Areas: No crimes in Fire Station, LEO spawns, or DOT station.
Rule 21 | Use of Uniforms & Liveries: Official liveries and uniforms only.
Rule 22 | Yielding to Staff Cars: If a staff car has lights on behind you, pull over immediately.
Rule 23 | Display Names: Realistic character names only. No staff rank impersonation.
Rule 24 | Respawning: Wait 2 mins for EMS. If LEO involved, call mod for heal to resume custody.

Official Terms of Service

Rule 1 | Respect & Conduct: Treat all members with respect; no harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks. Trolling or provoking conflict is prohibited.
Rule 2 | Appropriate Content: No NSFW, explicit, suggestive, or graphic/illegal material. Usernames and profiles must remain appropriate.
Rule 3 | Chat & Voice: No excessive profanity, slurs, or filter bypassing. No spamming, flooding, or mic abuse/screaming in VCs.
Rule 4 | Media & AI Integrity: No deepfakes or AI-generated media to mock/impersonate. No edited screenshots or spreading false rumors.
Rule 5 | Advertising: No self-promotion or advertising in DMs or channels without staff approval. Includes links and indirect invites.
Rule 6 | Staff Authority: Follow staff instructions; do not argue moderation in public. Use the ticket system for appeals or private concerns.
Rule 7 | Pings & Mentions: Do not ping @everyone, @here, or large roles. Ghost-pinging or bypassing mention restrictions is forbidden.
Rule 8 | Privacy & Safety: No doxxing, sharing personal info, or encouraging self-harm. Report safety concerns immediately via ticket.
Rule 9 | Exploits & Bribery: No abusing bots/permissions. Attempting to bribe staff results in an immediate ban and permanent blacklist.
Rule 10 | Leaving the Server: Leaving results in permanent loss of economy data and roles. Assets will not be restored except via successful ban appeal.
Rule 11 | Redirecting IA/HR+: Do not ping or redirect IA, HR+, or Leadership to gain faster responses. Only do so if instructed.
Rule 12 | External Behavior: HRPB may moderate behavior outside the server if it significantly harms the community or community leadership.
Rule 13 | Alt Accounts: Using alternate accounts to bypass bans, mutes, or economy limits is strictly prohibited.
Rule 14 | Toxicity & Mini-Modding: Do not act as staff if you don't hold the role. Toxic negativity that ruins the environment will be moderated.
Rule 15 | Trade & Scamming: Scams involving economy data, roles, or cross-trading for real money are forbidden. Staff are not responsible for unofficial trades.
Rule 16 | Common Sense Clause: Not all situations are listed; disruptive behavior is subject to staff discretion at any time.

Staff Guidelines

✔ Rule 1: Staff are required to utilize spelling, punctuation and grammar at all times. (This includes capitalizing the start of sentences and putting periods at the ends of sentences).

✔ Rule 2: Staff must always maintain professionalism and respect for others.

✗ Rule 3: Staff cannot abuse the :h and :m commands. Only use these commands for announcements.

✔ Rule 4: Staff must always use four or more letter commands. (e.g. :view Jydx ✔ :kick jX ✗).

✔ Rule 5: Staff must respond to mod calls. Failure to do so is considered a neglect of duty.

✗ Rule 6: Staff may NOT use kill-logs as proof in an RDM situation. We only take clips, screenshots, and recordings.

✔ Rule 7: Staff must always use correct uniforms and liveries.

✔ Rule 8: All staff should know the rules and review all staff documents regularly.

✔ Rule 9: Staff must have the correct callsign including their rank and random numbers (e.g., JM-120).

✗ Rule 10: Staff may not go AFK on duty, neither can they RP on-duty.

✔ Rule 11: Staff must take proper measures to maintain a safe environment.

✗ Rule 12: Staff cannot be disrespectful to anyone. SHR and HR disrespect leads to logged warnings.

✔ Rule 13: Staff must use common sense. Just because a rule isn't on here, doesn't mean you can do it.

✗ Rule 14: Staff cannot abuse their commands in an unfair way that ruins the roleplay experience.

✗ Rule 15: Staff cannot use commands off duty unless they have the specific role or are Ownership+.

✗ Rule 16: Staff must use 7+ letters of their username for kick or ban.

ERLC Private Server Rules

Rule 1 | FRP: All roleplays must withhold a realistic standard and you must not do anything that could be deemed as unrealistic.
Rule 2 | VDM: Vehicle deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot other vehicles without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 3 | RDM: Random Deathmatch is not allowed. This means you are not to shoot or kill anyone without a valid reason to do so.
Rule 4 | Gun Motion: You must use Gun Motion such as "grabs gun" or "slings" for every weapon apart from the pistol or taser. Law enforcement do not need to use gun motion at all.
Rule 5 | Realistic Avatars: Any morphs of your heads, bodies etc are not allowed.
Rule 6 | New Life Rule: Once your character is arrested or dies then you have no previous member as that character.
Rule 7 | Team Creation: Smaller teams like CIA, FBI etc. isn't allowed. Apply in discord.
Rule 8 | Realistic Driving: Drive realistically and roleplay all of your crashes no matter the speed.
Rule 9 | Fear RP: If someone is pointing a weapon at you, you must comply with their demands.
Rule 10 | Detain Requests: You must always accept detain requests. If unjustified, call staff afterwards.
Rule 11 | Pointless Open Carrying: Carrying a gun around for attention isn't allowed.
Rule 12 | Cop Baiting: Strictly not allowed. Do not start pointless pursuits.
Rule 13 | Robberies: 5 Minute Wait Time. Bank robberies require staff permission.
Rule 14 | Interrupting Roleplays: Do not interfere with on-going role plays.
Rule 15 | Priorities: No criminal activity that makes you wanted during priorities.
Rule 16 | Initiating Pursuits: Must always have a valid reason to initiate a pursuit.
Rule 17 | Group Limits: Max 4 for crimes, max 3 for Jewelry store.
Rule 18 | SWAT Gear: Not allowed if you aren't trained in SWAT.
Rule 19 | VC: If VC startup is selected, you must be in Discord VC.
Rule 20 | Safe Areas: No crimes in Fire Station, LEO spawns, or DOT station.
Rule 21 | Use of Uniforms & Liveries: Official liveries and uniforms only.
Rule 22 | Yielding to Staff Cars: If a staff car has lights on behind you, pull over immediately.
Rule 23 | Display Names: Realistic character names only. No staff rank impersonation.
Rule 24 | Respawning: Wait 2 mins for EMS. If LEO involved, call mod for heal to resume custody.
